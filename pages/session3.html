What is Rational Emotive & Congnitive Behavioral Therapy?

Rational-Emotive & Cognitive Behavioral Therapy was founded by psychologist Dr. Albert Ellis, in 1955 in New York, USA. It is the first Cognitive Behavioral approach which is an effective, evidence-based psychotherapy in dealing with a wide variety of emotional and behavioral difficulties.

Why choose such a therapy?

Our RECBT therapists will show you a useful way for managing your emotions and your behaviors. More specifically, the RECBT therapist will help you to locate and challenge your irrational (non-helpful) beliefs. Identifying and challenging non-helpful core beliefs will help you to feel and act better without being totally influenced from events around you.
